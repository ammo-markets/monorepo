---
title: State Restrictions
description: States where ammunition shipping is restricted and why.
---

While online ammunition sales are legal federally, some states impose additional requirements that currently prevent direct-to-consumer shipping from AmmoMarkets.

## Restricted States

| State | Restriction | Details |
|-------|------------|---------|
| **California (CA)** | Background check required | Must ship to a licensed dealer for in-store pickup |
| **Connecticut (CT)** | Permit required | Handgun ammo requires a permit; online sale restrictions |
| **Washington D.C.** | Effectively prohibited | Civilian ammunition purchases are extremely restricted |
| **Hawaii (HI)** | Permit required | Ammunition purchase requires a valid permit |
| **Illinois (IL)** | FOID card required | Must present a valid Firearm Owners Identification card |
| **Massachusetts (MA)** | FID/LTC required | Requires Firearms Identification or License to Carry |
| **New Jersey (NJ)** | FID required | Handgun ammo requires Firearms Purchaser ID; restricted online sales |
| **New York (NY)** | Dealer pickup required | Must ship to a licensed dealer for in-store pickup |

## States Where We Ship Today

All other U.S. states in the contiguous 48 support direct-to-consumer ammunition shipping. If your state isn't listed above, you can redeem and receive shipments.

**Not currently supported**: Alaska and Hawaii (due to shipping carrier restrictions — UPS Ground only).

## Dealer Partnerships (Coming Soon)

We're working on partnerships with licensed dealers in restricted states to enable legal redemptions. When ready, users in these states will be able to:

1. Redeem tokens on-chain
2. Select a partner dealer near them
3. Pick up their ammunition after completing the state-required checks

This feature is not yet available.
