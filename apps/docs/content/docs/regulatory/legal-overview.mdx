## Federal Law

At the **federal level**, no Federal Firearms License (FFL) is required to sell ammunition. Online ammunition sales are legal across the United States.

## Token Classification

Ammunition tokens are **commodity receipts** — each token represents a claim on one physical round of ammunition stored in a warehouse. They are backed 1:1 by real inventory, making them fundamentally different from speculative tokens or securities.

## State Laws

While federal law permits online ammunition sales, several states impose additional restrictions that prevent direct-to-consumer shipping. Redemption is available in the **39 U.S. states** without shipping restrictions. See [State Restrictions](/regulatory/state-restrictions) for details.

## What This Means for Users

- **Minting tokens**: No restrictions — purely on-chain
- **Trading tokens**: No restrictions — standard ERC-20 transfers
- **Redeeming for physical ammo**: Available in 39 U.S. states without shipping restrictions
