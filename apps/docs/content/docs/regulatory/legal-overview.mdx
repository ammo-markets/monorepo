## Federal Law

At the **federal level**, no Federal Firearms License (FFL) is required to sell ammunition. Online ammunition sales are legal across the United States, with age restrictions as the primary federal requirement.

## Age Requirements

Federal law sets minimum age requirements for ammunition purchases:

| Ammunition Type | Minimum Age |
|----------------|-------------|
| Handgun ammunition (e.g., 9mm) | 21 years old |
| Rifle/Shotgun ammunition (e.g., 5.56 NATO) | 18 years old |

Ammo Exchange enforces age verification during the redemption process.

## Token Classification

Ammunition tokens are **commodity receipts** — each token represents a claim on one physical round of ammunition stored in a warehouse. They are backed 1:1 by real inventory, making them fundamentally different from speculative tokens or securities.

## State Laws

While federal law permits online ammunition sales, several states impose additional restrictions that affect whether we can ship to your address. See [State Restrictions](/regulatory/state-restrictions) for details.

## What This Means for Users

- **Minting tokens**: No restrictions — purely on-chain
- **Trading tokens**: No restrictions — standard ERC-20 transfers
- **Redeeming for physical ammo**: Age verification required at delivery, some states restricted
