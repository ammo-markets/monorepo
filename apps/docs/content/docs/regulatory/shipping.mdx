## Carrier

All ammunition shipments go via **UPS Ground**. This is not a preference — it's a requirement:

- **USPS** prohibits shipping ammunition through the mail
- **FedEx** has restricted ammunition shipments
- **UPS Ground** is the standard carrier for commercial ammunition shipments

Air freight is not used for ammunition shipments.

## Coverage

- **39 U.S. states** — states without shipping restrictions
- **No international shipping** — U.S. domestic only
- **No P.O. boxes** — UPS Ground requires a physical street address

See [State Restrictions](/regulatory/state-restrictions) for the full list of restricted states.

## Packaging Requirements

All shipments follow DOT (Department of Transportation) regulations:

- **Corrugated cardboard boxes** — sturdy outer packaging
- **Limited Quantity labeling** — diamond-shaped marking per DOT 49 CFR
- **No air eligibility** — packages are marked ground-only
- **Weight limits** per package per carrier requirements

## Delivery Timeframes

UPS Ground delivery depends on distance from the warehouse:

| Distance | Estimated Delivery |
|----------|-------------------|
| Same region | 2–3 business days |
| Cross-region | 3–5 business days |
| Coast to coast | 5–7 business days |

These are estimates. Actual delivery times may vary based on UPS operations.

## Tracking

Once your order ships, tracking information appears in your [Portfolio](/guides/portfolio). You'll see the UPS tracking number and can follow your shipment's progress.
