## Fee Schedule

| Action | Fee | Details |
|--------|-----|---------|
| **Minting** | 1.5% | Deducted from your deposit |
| **Redeeming** | 0% | No fee on token redemption |
| **Holding** | 0% | No storage or decay fees — your tokens don't lose value over time |
| **Shipping** | At cost | Charged at redemption based on weight and destination |

## Mint Fee Example

When you deposit stablecoins to mint tokens:

1. You deposit **$100.00 USDT**
2. The 1.5% fee is deducted: **$1.50**
3. Net amount used for minting: **$98.50**
4. At a price of $0.30 per round, you receive approximately **328 tokens**

The fee is displayed in the review step before you confirm the transaction, so you always know the exact cost before minting.

## No Hidden Fees

- **No storage fees** — your tokens represent a claim on physical ammo that's already paid for. There's no ongoing cost.
- **No transfer fees** — sending tokens between wallets costs only the standard network gas fee (typically under $0.10).
- **No trading fees** — when DEX trading launches, you'll pay only the DEX's swap fee, not an additional protocol fee.

## Shipping Costs

When you redeem tokens for physical ammunition, shipping is charged at cost:

- **Carrier**: UPS Ground (the only carrier that ships ammunition)
- **Coverage**: 48 contiguous U.S. states
- **Cost**: Varies by weight and destination — displayed before you confirm your shipping address
