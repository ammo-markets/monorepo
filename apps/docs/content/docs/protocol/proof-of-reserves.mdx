## The Concept

Every ammunition token issued by AmmoMarkets is backed 1:1 by physical ammunition in our warehouse. Proof of Reserves is the mechanism that verifies this backing is maintained at all times.

## On-Chain Supply Verification

The total supply of each token is publicly verifiable on-chain at any time. Anyone can query the smart contracts to see exactly how many tokens exist for each caliber.

## Building Trust

Initially, trust is built the most direct way possible — by reliably fulfilling redemptions and shipping physical ammunition to users. Every successful delivery demonstrates that the backing is real.

## Future: Third-Party Audits

As the protocol grows, independent third-party audits of warehouse inventory are an ideal next step. This would include:

- **Physical counts** of warehouse inventory by an independent auditor
- **Attestation reports** confirming physical supply meets or exceeds on-chain token supply
- **Public dashboard** displaying real-time data on token supply vs. warehouse inventory

## Precedents

This model follows established patterns in tokenized commodities:

- **XAUT (Tether Gold)** — each token backed by physical gold in a Swiss vault
- **PAXG (Pax Gold)** — London Good Delivery gold bars, audited monthly
