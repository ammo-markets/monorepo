---
title: Get Stablecoins
description: How to get USDT or other stablecoins for minting ammunition tokens.
---

You need a supported stablecoin to mint ammunition tokens. Currently, **USDT (Tether)** is the primary payment token. Support for **USDC** and other stablecoins is planned.

## Buy from an Exchange

You can purchase USDT directly from centralized exchanges:

- **Binance** — the largest exchange for USDT trading pairs
- **Coinbase**, **Kraken**, or other major exchanges — buy USDT or swap from another asset

After purchasing, withdraw USDT to your wallet on the correct network. The app will guide you on which network to use.

## Bridge from Another Chain

If you already have USDT on another chain, you can bridge it to the correct network using cross-chain bridges like:

- **[Stargate](https://stargate.finance/)** — cross-chain bridge supporting multiple networks
- **[LayerZero](https://layerzero.network/)** — omnichain interoperability protocol

Bridging typically takes 5–15 minutes depending on the source chain.

## On-Ramp Providers

Buy crypto directly with a credit or debit card:

- **MoonPay**, **Transak**, or **Banxa** — purchase stablecoins directly into your wallet without needing an exchange account.

## Testnet

If you're testing on the testnet:

1. Get testnet tokens from the appropriate network faucet
2. The AmmoMarkets testnet includes a stablecoin faucet — visit the app and use the faucet button to get test tokens
