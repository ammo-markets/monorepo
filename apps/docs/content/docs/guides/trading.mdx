## The Concept

Ammunition tokens are standard **ERC-20 tokens**. Once liquidity pools are live, you'll be able to trade them freely on decentralized exchanges — just like any other token.

## Where You'll Be Able to Trade

Supported DEXs will be announced as liquidity pools are seeded. Any EVM-compatible DEX on the same network will work.

## Why Trade?

Trading on DEXs enables:

- **Price exposure without physical possession** — gain or lose value as ammo prices move, without storing physical rounds
- **Instant liquidity** — swap tokens for stablecoins or other assets at any time
- **No identity verification** — trading is purely on-chain, no sign-in or personal information needed

## International Users

International users will be able to **buy and trade** ammunition tokens on DEXs, but **cannot redeem** for physical delivery (U.S. shipping only). This opens up ammunition as a commodity investment for a global audience.
