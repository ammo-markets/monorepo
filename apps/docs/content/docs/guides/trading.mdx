## The Concept

Ammunition tokens are standard **ERC-20 tokens**. This means they're compatible with any decentralized exchange (DEX), lending protocol, or DeFi application on the same network — just like any other token.

## How to Trade

You can trade ammunition tokens on any EVM-compatible DEX. Simply connect your wallet to a DEX, select the ammunition token you want to trade, and swap it for stablecoins or other assets.

Popular DEXs on Avalanche include:

- **Trader Joe** — the largest DEX on Avalanche
- **Pangolin** — Avalanche-native DEX with deep liquidity
- **Uniswap** — available on many EVM chains

No account or identity verification is needed — trading is purely on-chain.

## Why Trade on DeFi?

Trading on DEXs enables:

- **Price exposure without physical possession** — gain or lose value as ammo prices move, without storing physical rounds
- **Instant liquidity** — swap tokens for stablecoins or other assets at any time
- **No identity verification** — trading is purely on-chain, no sign-in or personal information needed
- **Composability** — use ammunition tokens in DeFi protocols: provide liquidity, use as collateral, or build automated strategies
- **24/7 markets** — trade any time, unlike traditional ammunition retailers

## Providing Liquidity

If you hold ammunition tokens and stablecoins, you can provide liquidity on a DEX and earn trading fees from other users' swaps. This is a way to earn yield on your ammunition token holdings.

## International Users

International users can **buy and trade** ammunition tokens on DEXs, but **cannot redeem** for physical delivery (U.S. shipping only). This opens up ammunition as a commodity investment for a global audience.
