---
title: Portfolio & Order Tracking
description: View your token holdings, track orders, and monitor your portfolio.
---

The Portfolio page gives you a complete view of your ammunition token holdings and order history.

## Token Holdings

Your portfolio displays:

- **Each caliber you hold** with the current token balance (number of rounds)
- **Price per round** based on the latest oracle price
- **Total value** of each holding in USD
- **Total portfolio value** across all calibers
- **Stablecoin balance** in your wallet
- Quick links to **Mint** or **Redeem** for each caliber

If your wallet isn't connected, you'll see a prompt to connect.

## Active Orders

Any pending or in-progress orders appear in the active orders section:

- **Order type** — Mint or Redeem
- **Caliber** and amount
- **Status badge** — Pending or Processing
- **Timestamp** — when the order was created
- Click any order to view full details

## Order History

A filterable log of all your past transactions:

- **Filter by type** — All, Mint, Redeem, or Failed
- **Filter by caliber** — narrow to a specific ammunition type
- **Columns**: Type, Caliber, Amount, Status, Date
- Click any row to view the full order detail page
- Paginated — loads 10 orders at a time

## Order Detail Page

Click any order to see:

- **Visual progress stepper** showing the order's journey
- **Order summary** — caliber, amount, price, fees
- **Transaction details** — on-chain order ID, tx hash (links to Snowtrace explorer)
- **Shipping address** (for redemptions)
- **Tracking ID** (once shipped)

## Order Statuses

| Status | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Pending** | Transaction confirmed on-chain. Awaiting review. |
| **Processing** | Order accepted. Being prepared for fulfillment. |
| **Completed** | Order finalized. Tokens minted or ammo shipped. |
| **Failed** | Order could not be fulfilled. |
| **Cancelled** | Order was cancelled — tokens returned to your wallet. |
