---
title: Market Data
description: View live prices, charts, and supply data for all ammunition tokens.
---

The Market page shows real-time pricing and supply data for all available ammunition tokens.

## Current Prices

Each caliber displays:

- **Current price per round** in USD (from the oracle)
- **Price change** over the selected time period
- **Last updated** timestamp

## Price History Charts

Interactive charts show price movement over time. Available time ranges:

- **1 Day (1D)** — hourly price data
- **7 Days (7D)** — daily price data for the past week
- **30 Days (30D)** — daily data for the past month
- **90 Days (90D)** — daily data for the past quarter

## Supply Information

For each caliber, you can see:

- **Total supply** — total tokens in circulation
- **Current backing** — physical rounds in the warehouse

## Mint from Market

You can mint tokens directly from the Market page — click the mint button next to any caliber to jump straight into the minting flow with that caliber pre-selected.
